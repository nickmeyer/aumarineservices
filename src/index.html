AU Marine Services - Marine Vessel Maintenance and Repair

AU Marine Services offers the convenience of professional technician availability and prompt service. Set up maintenance around your schedule and AU Marine Services will dispatch trained, qualified and dedicated technicians to your vessel on time ready to exceed expectations. All our technicians are certified in their field of expertise, and have state of the art equipment allowing them to perform any job and keep a complete online set of records for all repairs and maintenance done, including before and after images.
WHAT WE DO.

Marine Services.
We provide mobile maintenance, installation, and repair services for marine vessels. We work on electronic installations, electrical systems, engines, generators, marine repairs, and much more.

HOW WE DO IT.

Expertise.

AU MARINE SERVICES TEAM

Justin Gross

Justin Gross started at Riviera Yachts in Australia in 1996 as a lamination specialist and over a 9 year period progressed into various other departments including engineering and fit out stages of the boat building process. In 2005, he relocated to Florida to work for Riviera Yachts of the Americas. As well as being the boatyard and custom projects manager, he also travelled and was involved in many specialized repairs and custom rigging of boats from the Americas to Europe.

Cameron Dewhurst

Cameron Dewhurst started in 2000 as a boatbuilding apprentice at Riviera Yachts in Australia working in various areas of lamination, cabinetry and research and development departments. In 2005 he relocated to Florida working for Riviera Yachts of the Americas traveling worldwide to perform various custom installations, service and warranty repairs. As an experienced Captain he has 1000’s of nautical miles delivering boats from the Pacific and Atlantic oceans including the Caribbean to the Mediterranean.

Jesse Fairbrother

Jesse Fairbrother started with AU Marine Services in 2012 coming from a background in custom audio and visual installations. Jesse is also highly skilled in lamination, painting and electrical installations and has been very versatile taking ownership of various large projects involving yacht customizations in the past 8+ years.
